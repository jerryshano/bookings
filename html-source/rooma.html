room a

Clearview Carehomes is an assisted care facility for the elderly. We serve seniors from 60 years old and up. We specialize in providing activities of daily living. Our staff and the administrator go above and beyond in caring for the everyday needs of our residents. The staff cater to the individual and unique needs of our residents. Our home cooked meals are based on our residents choice. The owner/licensee knows the appropriate nutritional needs for those residents who are on special diet such as diabetes, high blood pressure medications, heart failure and many other dietary requirements. Clearview Carehome is licensed for 6 beds. Our level of personal care and attention makes us unique.
